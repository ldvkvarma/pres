About Us

The Entity Studios is a global animation partner founded in 2024 by a team of AAA veterans with deep experience across gameplay, cinematics, and stylized pipelines.

Our artists have contributed to industry-defining titles like GTA V, Red Dead Redemption II, and Ghost Recon Breakpoint. Now we bring that same level of craft and care to collaborations across all genres and platforms.

We specialize in high-quality character animation, rigging, and motion capture services. Whether it's for social games, cinematic storytelling, or mobile-first experiences — our work blends performance with polish to deliver animation that feels responsive, expressive, and production-ready.

Built by animators for animation, our studio culture prioritizes creative ownership, technical excellence, and collaborative energy. We don’t just deliver animation — we plug directly into your pipeline and help bring your characters to life.
